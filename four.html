I am not religious, and thus don't have a set of guiding principles to rely on. So, I've decided to keep an evolving collection of books, which I so creatively call the "4 Books". I keep these on my desk and spend a few minutes each day paging re-reading pages as necessary. As my priorities change, so will the layout of my 4 Books.

4 Books

1. Meditations by Marcus Aurelius

2. The Heart of the Buddha's Teaching by Thich Nhat Hanh

3. The 48 Laws of Power by Robert Greene

4. How to Win Friends & Influence People by Dale Carnegie
